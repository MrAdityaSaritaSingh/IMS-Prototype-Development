# User Roles & Permissions - IMS Platform

This document defines the three key administrative roles within the Institute Management System (IMS), reflecting the standard placement workflow in Indian institutes.

## 1. Placement Admin (College Staff)
**Role Type:** Super Administrator / System Owner  
**Who:** Official college staff member or faculty in charge of the Placement Cell.

### Core Responsibilities
*   **Ecosystem Oversight:** Responsible for the entire placement process, from policy formulation to final statistics.
*   **System Configuration:** Manages academic years, placement policies, and global settings.
*   **User Management:** Has the authority to create users, approve SPC selections, and manage staff access.
*   **Escalation Handling:** Resolves disputes and handles sensitive issues that SPCs cannot manage.
*   **Audit & Compliance:** Full access to audit logs and historical data.

### Permissions
*   ✅ **Full Admin Rights:** Create/Read/Update/Delete (CRUD) access to all modules.
*   ✅ **Approvals:** Final authority on approving drives and sensitive data changes.

---

## 2. SPC — Student Placement Coordinator (Batch-Level)
**Role Type:** Operational Manager  
**Who:** Final-year student selected to represent their batch.

### Core Responsibilities
*   **Operational Backbone:** Executes the day-to-day activities of the placement cell.
*   **Drive Management:** Manages multiple companies and drives simultaneously.
*   **Student Support:** Primary point of contact for student queries and grievances.
*   **Workflow Management:** Handles scheduling, shortlisting, and offer release workflows.
*   **Liaison:** Works closely with the Placement Admin to implement policies.

### Permissions
*   ✅ **Extended Rights:** Can manage drives, students, and applications.
*   ❌ **Restricted:** Cannot change system-level configurations or access sensitive audit logs without authorization.

---

## 3. SPOC — Student Point of Contact (Company-Specific)
**Role Type:** Liaison / Task-Specific Coordinator  
**Who:** A student appointed specifically to handle *one* company.

### Core Responsibilities
*   **Bridge:** Acts as the single point of communication between the Recruiter and the Institute for a specific drive.
*   **Logistics:** Coordinates interview schedules, hospitality, and on-ground logistics for their assigned company.
*   **Information Collection:** Gathers requirements and updates from the recruiter.
*   **Query Handling:** Answers student queries specific to their assigned company.

### Permissions
*   ✅ **Scoped Access:** Can only view and manage data related to their **assigned company/drive**.
*   ❌ **No Global Access:** Cannot see or influence other drives or the broader placement ecosystem.
